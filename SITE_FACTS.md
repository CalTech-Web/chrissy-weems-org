## Initiatives & Advocacy

### 1. Force for Good
- Origami Owl's philanthropic venture
- Donated $1.15 million to charitable causes in 2016
- Supports academic opportunities for children
- Champions children's welfare and support
- Economic empowerment of women in developing nations
- Partners with more than a dozen charitable groups in Arizona

### 2. I Am Bullyproof Campaign
- Anti-bullying video campaign
- Features awareness content and resources
- Partnered with organizations fighting childhood bullying

### 3. Origami Owl + Childhelp Partnership
- Partnership to save children's lives
- Raises awareness for child welfare
- Special collections to benefit the cause

### 4. Owlette Program
- Youth entrepreneurship program for ages 11 to 17
- Youth work alongside a parent who is an Origami Owl Designer
- Sharpens entrepreneurial and leadership skills
- Builds confidence in young people

### 5. Live Sparkly
- An inspiring collection of stories featuring Origami Owl and the Owlettes
- Motivational storytelling platform
- Website: livesparkly.com
